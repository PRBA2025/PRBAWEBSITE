PHILIPPINE ROLL BALL HISTORY

March 18, 2023: Official Launch

The Muntinlupa Roll Ball Association was officially launched at Decathlon, Alabang.

April 3, 2023 – World Cup Prep

Preparations began for the Roll Ball World Cup to be held in Pune, India from April 21-26, 2023.

April 24, 2023 – TV Feature

The Philippine Roll Ball Association was featured with Prof. Bonifacio Escoda during the taping of AHA!.

September 29, 2023 – Webinar Launch

Introducing Roll Ball, a free webinar to introduce the sport.

October 6, 2023 (2:00 PM – 4:00 PM) – Soft Launch

Soft launch of Roll Ball in the Philippines at Decathlon, Muntinlupa City.

October 14, 15, 20, 21, 2023 – Virtual Seminar Series

Virtual seminar series conducted to further promote Roll Ball

June 17, 20, 22, 2024 – Practice Sessions

Roll Ball practice sessions held at the Mother of Perpetual Help School Fairview Covered Gymnasium in Quezon City.

June 23, 2024 – First Exhibition Game

The first Roll Ball exhibition game took place in the Philippines.

August 29, 2024 – POC Application

PRBA applied for membership in the Philippine Olympic Committee (POC) as a National Sports Association.

September 29, 2024 – DZME Interview

Interview on DZME featuring the PRBA for wider media exposure.

September 30, 2024 – Token Presentation

PRBA President and Executive Director Ptr. Tony Ortega presented a token of appreciation to Mr. Amitabh Sharma, Chairman of GTCC and member of the India Olympic Association Executive Committee, at Lanson Place, Mall of Asia.

October 2, 2024 – Activities Related to Mr. Raju Dabhade

Activities related to Mr. Raju Dabhade took place (please clarify the context for this entry).

October 12, 19, 26, 2024 – Player Tryouts

Calls for player tryouts in Roll Ball.

October 13, 2024 – MOA Signing

Signing of a Memorandum of Agreement establishing Roller Disco as the official home court for PRBA’s tryouts, training sessions, and future events.

December 16–19, 2024 – 4th Asian Roll Ball Championship

The 4th Asian Roll Ball Championship was held in the Philippines.
